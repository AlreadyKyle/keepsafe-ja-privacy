Privacy Policy

2026-04-24

Fuqiang UG (haftungsbeschränkt) ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Himitsu mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device: photos and videos you import or capture within the App, your PIN and authentication preferences, album names and organizational structure, and app settings and preferences.

All of this data is encrypted using AES-256-CBC with HMAC-SHA256 integrity verification and remains on your device at all times. We do not have access to your photos, videos, PIN, or any content stored in the App.

2. Anonymous Usage Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics. This includes app launch and session duration, feature usage patterns (e.g., which screens are visited), device type and operating system version, app version, and crash reports.

This data does not include any personally identifiable information (PII). We cannot identify individual users from analytics data. No photos, videos, file names, or personal content is ever transmitted.

3. Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier (not linked to your identity) and purchase/subscription status.

We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect:

- No personal information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier.
- No account registration: The App does not require or offer account creation.
- No cloud storage: Your photos and videos are never uploaded to any server.
- No location data: We do not access or store your location.
- No contacts: We do not access your address book.
- No advertising data: We do not collect data for advertising purposes.
- No biometric data: Biometric authentication (Face ID / fingerprint) is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We never receive, process, or store biometric data.
- No cross-app tracking: We do not track you across other apps or websites.

5. Device Permissions

Camera: Used to capture photos and videos directly into the encrypted vault. Photos are encrypted immediately and stored only on your device. The front camera may also capture a photo when an incorrect PIN is entered (break-in alert feature). These photos are encrypted and stored only on your device.

Photo Library: Used to import existing photos and videos into the encrypted vault. Original photos in your device's photo library are not modified or deleted by the App.

Biometric Authentication: Used to provide Face ID or fingerprint unlock. Biometric authentication is processed entirely by your device's secure enclave. The App receives only a success/failure result and never accesses the underlying biometric data.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All photos and videos are encrypted using AES-256-CBC with HMAC-SHA256 integrity verification. Encryption keys are derived from your PIN and stored in your device's secure storage. Data is encrypted at rest and is only decrypted temporarily for display.

Important: Because all data is stored locally and encrypted with your PIN, we cannot recover your data if you forget your PIN. There is no "forgot PIN" or account recovery mechanism.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All App data is stored exclusively on your device. You can delete individual photos and videos from within the App. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all App data from your device, including all photos, videos, and settings. We do not retain any copies of your data because we never receive it.

8. Third-Party Services

PostHog (Analytics): Collects anonymous usage data to help us improve the App. Privacy policy: https://posthog.com/privacy

RevenueCat (Purchase Management): Manages subscription and purchase status. Privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Processes all in-app purchases and subscriptions, subject to their respective privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Japan, including the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI). For users outside Japan, local consumer protection laws may also apply to the extent they provide additional protections.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and the "Last updated" date will be revised. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
